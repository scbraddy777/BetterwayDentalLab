A Clear Workflow From Intake to Delivery

Each step is designed to keep submissions organized, production aligned, and return timing easier to plan around.

Case Intake

Submit digital scans or traditional impressions with prep notes, shade details, and delivery expectations.

Clinical Review

Each case is reviewed for fit, occlusion, esthetics, and production readiness before fabrication begins.

Production

Milling, finishing, and quality control follow a consistent process designed to support fit, esthetics, and dependable results.

Quality Check & Delivery

Final checks are completed before shipment to help support a smoother delivery.
