SERVICES

Core Services

Each service line is supported by practical case review, disciplined production, and clear turnaround expectations.

Crowns & Bridges

Single crowns and bridge cases supported with monolithic and layered options chosen around indication, strength, and esthetic goals.

  • Posterior and anterior crown indications
  • Single units and multi-unit bridgework
  • Prep and material considerations when questions come up

Implant Crowns

Implant crown cases coordinated around restorative design, component details, and seating predictability.

  • Screw-retained and cement-retained solutions
  • Custom abutment coordination when needed
  • Review of implant system details before fabrication

Veneers

Veneer workflows centered on shade planning, contour refinement, and natural-looking esthetics.

  • Layered esthetic approach for smile-zone cases
  • Shade and translucency planning support
  • Prep and seating guidance for predictable results

Case Planning Support

Support for cases that need extra review before production, including occlusion, sequencing, and material selection.

  • Case-specific recommendations before fabrication
  • Timing guidance when multiple steps are involved
  • Practical input when complexity increases

Turnaround by Case Type

Estimated ranges below help practices plan scheduling; final timing depends on case specifics and approvals.

Service Estimated Turnaround Notes
Single-unit crown 5-7 business days Rush options available when feasible.
Bridge cases 7-10 business days Case review may adjust timeline.
Implant crown cases 7-12 business days Dependent on implant system details.
Veneer cases 5-8 business days Shade review and esthetic design approval may adjust timing.

Send over the case details and we can help clarify the right starting point, expected timeline, and next steps.
