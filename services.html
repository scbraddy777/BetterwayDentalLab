Service Overview

Restorative solutions designed to support your clinical goals and keep cases on schedule.

We understand that chair time is valuable. That's why, at Better Way Dental Lab, we strive for an average "five-minute crown seat." We put in the extra effort on our end to help your team work more efficiently, reduce delivery-day friction, and protect practice profitability.

Crowns & Bridges

Dependable crown and bridge solutions for both posterior and anterior cases, with the fit, contacts, and margins your practice can count on.

  • Zirconia and layered esthetic restorations
  • Single crowns and multi-unit bridges
  • Prep guidance to help support a smooth, predictable fit

Implant Crowns

Implant crown solutions designed to make restorative treatment feel more straightforward, with close coordination every step of the way.

  • Screw-retained and cement-retained implant crown options
  • Custom abutment coordination for improved restorative fit
  • Clear communication throughout each stage of the case

Veneers

Thoughtful veneer planning and fabrication focused on natural-looking esthetics, consistency, and a smooth process from start to finish.

  • Veneer planning support before fabrication begins
  • Layered esthetics for natural shade and translucency
  • Prep and seating guidance for more predictable results

Case Planning Support

Helpful technical support for challenging cases, with guidance on occlusion, esthetics, materials, and case sequencing.

  • Specific material recommendations based on the case
  • Turnaround and sequencing guidance
  • Support for more complex cases

Typical Turnaround Guidance

Final case timing depends on complexity and communication cycle requirements.

Service Estimated Turnaround Notes
Single-unit crown 5-7 business days Rush options available when feasible.
Bridge cases 7-10 business days Case review may adjust timeline.
Implant crown cases 7-12 business days Dependent on implant system details.
Veneer cases 5-8 business days Shade review and esthetic design approval may adjust timing.
