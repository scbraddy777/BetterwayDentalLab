Workflow Transparency

From intake to delivery, every case follows a controlled and communicative process.

Our workflow is designed to reduce remakes, improve scheduling confidence, and keep your clinical team informed.

Case Intake

Submit digital scans or traditional impressions with prep and shade details through our guided intake pathway.

Clinical Planning Review

Each case is checked for indications, occlusal expectations, contacts, and timeline feasibility before production.

Precision Production

Milling and finishing are managed through repeatable quality checkpoints, including fit and esthetic verification.

QC + Shipment

Final quality checks are completed before packaging and tracked shipment back to the practice.

Quality Control Standards

  • Margin and contact verification on all definitive units.
  • Material and shade documentation checkpoints.
  • Case-level communication when clarification is required.
  • Implant components confirmed before finalization.
  • Veneer esthetic approvals completed before finalization.
  • Shipment packaging and label validation before dispatch.

Shipping and Communication

Our team provides practical updates at key phases so practices can manage appointment flow effectively.

  • Tracked shipping for return cases.
  • Rush coordination for urgent restorative needs.
  • Dedicated contact channels for status updates.
